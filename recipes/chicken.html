Description

Transport your taste buds to a culinary paradise with our Parmesan Chicken Pizza. This mouthwatering masterpiece combines the best of two worlds, featuring a crispy chicken Parmesan topping with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella cheese. Whether you're a fan of classic Italian dishes or crave a unique culinary experience, our diverse range of recipes has something for everyone

Ingredients

  • 12 frozen chicken nuggets
  • 1 12-inch pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 2 button mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 6 basil leaves, cut into thin ribbons

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C). Arrange nuggets in a single layer on a pizza pan.
  2. Bake nuggets in the pre-heated oven for 10 minutes or according to package directions; transfer to a cutting board and cut each nugget in half..
  3. Increase the oven temperature to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  4. Place pizza crust on the pizza pan; spread on marinara in an even layer. Arrange halved nuggets and sliced mushrooms evenly over marinara; sprinkle with mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until heated through, about 5 minutes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and scatter basil over cheese; bake for 3 minutes more.
