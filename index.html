Description

Discover the irresistible charm of a homemade pepperoni pizza, a culinary masterpiece that will transport you to Italy's finest pizzerias. This delicious dish is a symphony of flavors and textures, featuring:

  • golden, crispy crust
  • Tangy tomato sauce
  • Gooey, melted mozzarella cheese
  • slightly crispy pepperoni slices

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 cup plus 3 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, plus more for greasing

Toppings

  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce, divided
  • 1 cup shredded low-moisture mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup drained and sliced pepperoncini salad peppers, or to taste
  • 12 slices pepperoni, or to taste
